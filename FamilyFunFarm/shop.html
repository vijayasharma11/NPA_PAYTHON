Fresh, Local & Delicious!

Discover the best of what Abbington Farm has to offer, from farm-fresh eggs to locally made pies. Here’s what you can expect in our shop:

  • Free-Range Eggs - Freshly laid by our own chickens.
  • Homemade Jams & Preserves - Sweet and tangy, just like Grandma used to make.
  • Locally Sourced Meats - Including award-winning pork and lamb.
  • Handmade Cakes & Breads - Freshly baked on-site every day.
  • Fresh Vegetables - Grown in our very own garden.

Don’t forget to check out our café for fresh homemade cakes, coffee, and more.
