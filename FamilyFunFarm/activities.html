Fun for the Whole Family

At Abbington Farm, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. From hands-on farm activities to seasonal fun, we’ve got it all!

  • Petting Zoo - Meet and feed our friendly animals.
  • Hayrides - Hop on for a scenic ride around the farm.
  • Seasonal Corn Maze - Get lost in our fun and challenging maze.
  • Pick-Your-Own Produce - Fresh fruits and veggies are ready for you to pick.
  • Farm Tools Play - Kids love getting hands-on with real farm tools.
  • Nature Walks - Explore the beauty of the countryside on foot.

Visit Us Today!

Whether you’re here for the animals, the produce, or the fun, Abbington Farm has something to offer.
