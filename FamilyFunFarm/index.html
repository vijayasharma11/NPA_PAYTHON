Your Perfect Stop Between Edinburgh and Carlisle

Whether you're looking for fresh local produce, a fun family day out, or just a relaxing spot to unwind, Abbington Farm has something for everyone. Explore our farm shop, enjoy a delicious treat at the café, or take part in our fun seasonal activities!

What We Offer

  • Farm Shop

    From free-range eggs to homemade jams, our farm shop offers a variety of fresh, local products.

  • Activities

    Meet our friendly farm animals, explore our seasonal mazes, or enjoy a hayride across our fields.

  • Café

    Relax with a cup of freshly brewed coffee and a slice of homemade cake, made right here on the farm.
